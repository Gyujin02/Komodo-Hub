Who We Serve
Three portals, one platform
Schools
School Portal
Principals register their school and subscribe. School admins generate access codes for teachers and students. Teachers create conservation programs and activities. Students submit work — all private from the public.
Register School →
Communities
Community Hub
Community organisations like #SaveOurAnimals register and contribute articles, essays, and wildlife sighting reports. Member profiles and contributions are publicly visible to showcase conservation work.

Foundation
Yayasan Komodo

How It Works
From registration to conservation
01
Register & Get Approved
School principal registers the school. Yayasan Komodo reviews and approves — the school becomes active on the platform.
02
Issue Access Codes
School admin generates unique access codes for teachers and students. Community admin creates codes for community members.
03
Create Programs
Teachers create conservation programs and activities — field studies, essays, sighting logs. Students access and submit their work.
04
Contribute to Conservation
Students and community members report endangered species sightings. School library published publicly — student profiles always private.
